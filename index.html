This is a collection of films made on the MA Animation programme at the Royal College of Art, now celebrating 40 years.

This site reads in a .csv file of public Vimeo links taken from the RCA Animation Vimeo Channel. Since Vimeo has blocked Collections, Channels and Staff Picks in the UK and EU, it now also exists here.

It's not a complete archive and links will inevitably break, but that's part of the charm.

Why not hit shuffle and hopefully find something new!
