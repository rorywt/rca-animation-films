what

This is a collection of films made on the MA Animation programme at the Royal College of Art, now celebrating 40 years.

It first existed as a Vimeo Channel. Since Vimeo has blocked Collections, Channels and Staff Picks in the UK and EU, it now also exists here.

This is not a complete archive, just the stuff that was already on Vimeo.

There is no way to search this collection. Just hit shuffle and hopefully find something new!
